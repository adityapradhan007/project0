Coronavirus (covid-19)

Q)What is a coronavirus?

This is a group of viruses which causes illness in humans and animals.In humans,it usually infects the

respiratory system and infections ranging from the common cold to more severe dieases such as MERS and SARS.

Q)How does covid-19 spread?

It usually spread when one infected person comes in contact with a healthy person therefore this virus is

highly contagious disease.The disease can spread from person to person through small dropletsfrom the nose or

mouth which are spread when person with the disease coughs or exhales

Q)Is there any cure for it?

Till now there is no cure for it.But you can prevent it by social distancing
